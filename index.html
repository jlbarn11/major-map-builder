Welcome!

This site was created to help you view and understand your options for programs and courses in the Graphic Information Technology program at Arizona State University. Below, you can find information about these topics, as well as an interactive major map builder (your major map helps you to understand which courses you should take and in which order).

This is not an official tool, and you are encouraged to reach out to the Polytechnic School Advising Department or a member of the faculty for the GIT Program with any questions you have.

To get started, use the links above or below to find more information about each program's requirements, courses offered, course topics, and additional details. You can also go directly to the major map builder if you are ready to look at a plan in more depth.

Major Map Builder

From the menus below, choose your program type (campus or online), the degree program you are interested in, and your primary focus area for that program (if applicable). The courses that are required will populate in the map builder below, and you can drag and drop additional courses from your focus area into the builder as well. When you are ready, feel free to print your generated major map to refer to as you complete the program or in preparation for your next appointment with an advisor.
